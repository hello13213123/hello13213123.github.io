Jett - Valorant Agent

About Jett

Jett is a South Korean agent in Valorant known for her agility and evasive maneuvers. She can quickly navigate the battlefield, making her a challenging target to hit. Jett's abilities allow her to outmaneuver opponents and execute precise attacks.

Gallery

Abilities

  • Cloudburst: Jett throws a projectile that expands into a brief vision-blocking cloud on impact.
  • Updraft: Jett propels herself upwards.
  • Tailwind: Jett dashes a short distance in the direction she is moving.
  • Blade Storm (Ultimate): Jett equips a set of highly accurate throwing knives that deal moderate damage and kill on headshots. Scoring a kill restores all daggers.
